About BingoPlus

BingoPlus is a digital platform designed to deliver interactive entertainment experiences through modern mobile technology. The platform focuses on bringing traditional bingo-style interaction into a digital environment that can be accessed through smartphones and web interfaces.

The platform was introduced in 2022 by AB Leisure Exponent Inc., a subsidiary of DigiPlus Interactive Corp. The service aims to provide a convenient mobile experience through optimized user interfaces and reliable system performance.
